## Summary
In this interview, Dwarkesh Patel speaks with Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang about the persistence of Nvidia's moat in the AI hardware landscape. They discuss the future of AI compute, the scaling of upstream supply chains like TSMC, and Nvidia's competitive advantages over custom ASICs and TPUs. The conversation also explores the geopolitical implications of exporting AI chips to China and Nvidia's strategic investments in the broader AI ecosystem.
