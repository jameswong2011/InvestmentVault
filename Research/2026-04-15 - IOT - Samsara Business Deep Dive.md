# Samsara Inc. (IOT) — Connected Operations Platform Deep Dive

## Key Takeaways

- **$1.9B ARR growing 30% YoY** with first GAAP-profitable quarter (Q3 FY2026, ~$9M net income). FCF margin reached 21% adjusted — firmly in "Rule of 40" territory.
- **Data flywheel is the durable moat**: 2M+ active devices generating 25T annual data points create self-reinforcing competitive advantages that cannot be replicated through capital investment alone.
- **Enterprise segment accelerating**: 2,990 customers at $100K+ ARR contributing $1.2B (63% of total), growing 37% YoY. $1M+ ARR customers growing 56% YoY.
- **Platform stickiness proven**: 95% of large customers subscribe to multiple products, 69% use 3+ products. NPS of 75.6 exceeds competitors by 12-44 points.
- **Geotab is primary competitor** but Samsara leads in customer satisfaction (84% vs 76%), G2 scores (99 vs 56.2), and AI/platform capabilities. ABI Research ranks Samsara #2 overall behind Geotab.

**Profitability inflection**: Net losses shrunk from $355M (FY2022) → $287M (FY2024) → ~$9M (FY2026). FCF swung from -$191M (FY2022) to +$207M (FY2026) — a ~60 percentage point FCF margin improvement in 4 years.

## Platform Architecture

Three-layer model creating integrated competitive advantages:

| Layer | Function | Economics | Strategic Value |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------------|
| IoT Devices | Data capture, edge inference | At/near cost, 77% HaaS adoption | Customer acquisition, switching costs |
| Cloud Platform | Data processing, storage, APIs | 75-78% gross margin, scaling efficiency | Recurring revenue, network effects |
| AI Analytics | Insights, automation, predictions | Differentiated value, premium pricing | Competitive moat, expansion driver |

## Enterprise Customer Metrics (Q3 FY2026)

- **2,990 customers** at $100K+ ARR (37% YoY growth)
- **$1.2B ARR** from enterprise segment (63% of total)
- **219 new $100K+ customers** added in Q3 — highest quarterly addition ever
- **95% multi-product adoption** among large customers
- **$1M+ ARR customers** growing 56% YoY
- **Q4 net new ARR**: $145M, up 33% YoY — highest growth rate in 8 quarters

## AI & Data Moat

- **AI Safety Coach**: Agentic AI automating entire safety workflows — evolution from analytical to operational AI
- **25 trillion annual data points** from diverse vehicle types, operating conditions, geographic contexts
- **75 billion API calls annually** demonstrating platform extensibility
- **HappyRobot partnership**: AI-powered voice automation for logistics communications — extending into agentic AI

## Geographic Distribution (FY2026)

- **United States**: 85.6% ($1.39B) — mature, high-value
- **International**: 14.4% ($233M) — management targeting 18% of revenue
- Key international markets: UK (~8%), France (~3%), Mexico (~2%)
- **Bengaluru R&D hub** established 2024 — Asia-Pacific beachhead

## Verticals Served

Construction, transportation/logistics, field services, manufacturing, retail, waste management, oil & gas, municipal/government fleets. Platform applicability across any industry with physical assets and workers requiring visibility and optimization.

## Key Risks

- **Valuation**: High-growth SaaS multiples require sustained 30%+ ARR growth
- **Geotab installed base**: 4.6M vehicles vs Samsara's 2M devices — scale disadvantage in telematics
- **International expansion execution**: 85.6% US concentration creates geographic risk
- **Hardware dependency**: Physical devices create capex intensity vs pure SaaS models (mitigated by 77% HaaS)
- **Enterprise concentration**: 63% of ARR from $100K+ customers — customer loss = material impact
