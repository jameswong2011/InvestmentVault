## Shopify Payments & Shop Pay

- Shopify Payments penetration: 68% of GPV (up from 56% in early 2023)
- 90% of eligible merchants have activated Shopify Payments
- Shop Pay: 200M+ users, 38% of GPV (up from 33% in 2023)
- 50% higher conversion than guest checkout
- ~1.89M live websites using Shop Pay (+14.2% YoY installs)

## Shopify Capital & Financial Services

- $4.2B in MCAs and business loans originated in FY2025
- $1.78B outstanding
- $258M lending revenue (up from $205M)

## Product Developments — Winter '26 "RenAIssance" Edition

**150+ new features including:**

1. **Agentic Storefronts**: Products discoverable inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot. Available to all merchants as of March 2026. AI-driven orders increased 15-fold in 2025.

2. **Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP)**: Co-developed with Google, announced at NRF 2026. Partnerships with Walmart, Target, 20+ retailers. Open standard for AI agent transactions covering full shopping journey (discovery → purchase → post-purchase). Competes with Stripe/OpenAI's ACP.

3. **Sidekick AI (rebuilt)**: Now a proactive agent. Pulse feature surfaces personalized, high-impact tasks. Natural language theme editing, automation building from plain-English descriptions. VP Vanessa Lee acknowledged original version "wasn't widely used by merchants" before 2025 re-architecture.

4. **Tinker App**: AI design tool launching early 2026. Solves "blank canvas problem" for creators. Professional visuals from no-design-experience starting point.

5. **B2B Tools for All Tiers**: Previously Plus-exclusive B2B features (company profiles, volume pricing, net payment terms) expanded to all merchant plans.

6. **Managed Markets Enhanced**: International compliance checks, faster payouts, full harmonization for global fulfillment.

## USDC Stablecoin Payments Integration

- Partnership: Shopify + Stripe + Coinbase
- Settlement: on Base (Coinbase L2 network) — lower costs, faster settlement
- Coverage: 34 countries
- Merchant incentive: up to 0.5% cash back on USDC orders
- Consumer incentive: unspecified % cash back planned
- USDT (Tether) support announced April 2026
- Multi-chain support: Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon
- Merchants can receive fiat or settle in USDC on-chain

## Competitive Landscape Updates

**Stripe:**
- $5.1B net revenue (2024), $1.9T TPV, $159B private valuation
- Revenue & Finance Automation Suite: $500M+ ARR, tracking toward $1B in 2026
- Lemon Squeezy (acquired July 2024): now building Stripe Managed Payments (35+ countries). User reports: extended onboarding, unclear roadmap, 10-18% fees with add-ons
- Metronome acquisition (Jan 2026): usage-based billing for AI companies
- ACP adopted by Squarespace, Wix, Etsy, WooCommerce, BigCommerce — all non-Shopify platforms
- Stripe endorsed UCP despite creating ACP — hedging

**TikTok Shop:**
- Global GMV: ~$33B (2024), roughly tripled from $11B (2023)
- US: $1B+/month for six consecutive months in H1 2025
- USDS joint venture resolved January 2026 (majority US ownership)
- Discovery-based commerce: DTC brands in beauty/fashion/wellness launching TikTok-first
- However: TikTok is a channel, not a storefront builder; many sellers still need Shopify

**BigCommerce (Commerce.com):**
- 2.8% revenue growth in 2025
- Net retention below 100%
- Declining enterprise accounts — clear competitive erosion

## Tariff & De Minimis Impact

**De Minimis Elimination:**
- Eliminated for China/HK May 2, 2025; expanded to all countries July 30, 2025 (effective August 29)
- 1 in 5 Shopify merchants used de minimis exemption
- 1/3 of those relied on it for 90%+ of shipments

**Current Tariff Rates:**
- 145% on Chinese imports
- 25% on Canadian and Mexican goods
- 10% baseline on imports from most other countries

**Supreme Court Ruling (Feb 2026):**
- Invalidated IEEPA-based tariffs
- Administration immediately signed new 10% Section 122 (Trade Act 1974) temp duty for 150 days
- Shopify stock rose 1% on ruling

## Agentic Commerce Protocol Comparison

| Feature | UCP (Shopify/Google) | ACP (Stripe/OpenAI) |
|---------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Focus | Full shopping journey: discovery → purchase → post-purchase | Checkout optimization: chat-to-buy |
| Backers | Shopify, Google, Walmart, Target, 20+ retailers | Stripe, OpenAI, Squarespace, Wix, Etsy, WooCommerce, BigCommerce |
| Commerce Model | Search-to-buy discovery | Chat-to-buy interactions |
| Merchant Type | Merchant-facing platforms | Infrastructure-focused |
| Implementation | /.well-known/ucp standard | API-based checkout integration |
| Key Insight | Dual UCP/ACP merchants capture **40% more** agentic traffic | Stripe endorsed UCP despite creating ACP |
