# Lumentum vs Coherent: Comprehensive Business and Investment Analysis

## Key Takeaways

- **"Great Photonic Divergence"**: LITE +1,098% vs COHR +328% 1-year stock performance (as of March 2026). Pure-play specialist vs vertically integrated conglomerate — fundamentally different value capture approaches.
- **LITE Q2 FY26**: $665.5M revenue (+65.5% YoY, +24.7% QoQ). Q3 FY26 guidance $780-830M implying ~85% YoY growth — acceleration, not deceleration.
- **COHR Q2 FY26**: $1.69B revenue (+17% YoY). Datacenter & Communications segment +34% to $1.21B (72% of total). Solid but measured vs LITE's explosive trajectory.
- **NVIDIA $2B investment in each** validates both approaches. LITE's pure-play EML focus vs COHR's vertically integrated one-stop capability each serve different hyperscaler needs.
- **Module-level gross margin**: LITE achieving 70.5% on select products — extraordinary for optical module industry, demonstrating pricing power from technical differentiation.

## Strategic Positioning

**Lumentum — Pure-Play AI Photonics Specialist:**
- EML (electro-absorption modulated laser) dominance — mission-critical for AI scale-out networks
- Record EML shipments, 200G-lane-speed devices comprising 10% of Datacom laser chip revenue despite only 5% of unit volume
- $400M+ OCS (optical circuit switch) backlog
- Multi-hundred-million-dollar CPO orders extending into 2027
- 50%+ planned capacity expansion with 40% increases scheduled
- New U.S. fabrication facility funded by NVIDIA partnership
- Components segment: $443.7M (66.7% of revenue, +68.3% YoY)
- Systems segment: $222M (+60% YoY)

**Coherent — Vertically Integrated Photonics Conglomerate:**
- End-to-end capabilities: silicon carbide wafer production → laser fabrication → finished transceiver modules
- ~25% market share in optical transceivers — volume leadership
- 6-inch InP wafer platform — manufacturing scale advantage
- Diversified across datacenter, industrial, automotive, materials
- One-stop supplier for hyperscalers requiring 500K+ unit deployments
- Revenue scale >2.5x Lumentum's quarterly run-rate
- SiC materials and industrial businesses provide revenue stability

## Investment Framework

**If scale-out networks with discrete optical components remain dominant** → LITE offers superior return potential (concentrated exposure to highest-value bottleneck).

**If integration trends accelerate toward CPO/converged architectures** → COHR's diversified capabilities may enable more effective adaptation.

**NVIDIA's dual investment** ($2B each + multi-billion purchase commitments) suggests the AI infrastructure architect sees value in both approaches — LITE's pure-play focus complementing COHR's integrated platform.
