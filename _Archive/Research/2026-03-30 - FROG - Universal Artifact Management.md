**Q:** What does JFrog do - Please provide a 500 word summary of artifacts and artifact management.

---

JFrog provides Artifactory, a universal artifact repository manager for DevOps. Artifacts are build outputs (JARs, Docker images, binaries, packages, containers, AI models). Artifact management centrally stores, versions, secures, distributes them for reproducibility, security, compliance, and software supply chain governance.
